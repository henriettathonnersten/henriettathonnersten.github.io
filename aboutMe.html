Final year project

PEAS - a Preparation of Exam papers & Assignment briefs System

September 2017 – May 2018

My final year project is a proof-reading system, that moves the process of proof-reading online and allows the author and proof-reader of a document to log on to a dedicated proof-reading system, instead of sending heavy word editor files that quickly fills up their inboxes.
PEAS handles documents containing text and images, allows for comments and approvals of separate sections of documents, creates PDFs, and notifies users via email when an update has been made to one of their documents, so they know when to log on and review it.
